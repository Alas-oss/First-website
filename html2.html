About us

Rather than just showing the presentation and doing the exercises, we first taught them the base principles of Hour of Code exercises. Aliya Alasgarova, Elmar Misirzada, Nigar Yusifova, and Urfan Zeynalov took on the task of planning an introductory course at Landau School. We tried to include about 15 year-six students in the coding experience despite an ongoing event that we were not told about.
